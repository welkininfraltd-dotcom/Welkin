Welkin Cash Tracker — Built by Welkin Builders Infrastructure Ltd

The smart way to manage daily cash expenses across your construction project sites. Designed for construction companies managing government road and infrastructure projects.

KEY FEATURES:

📝 Quick Cash Entry
Record vendor payments instantly — date, vendor, item, quantity, rate, amount. Auto-calculates totals. Capture invoice photos directly from your phone camera.

📦 Standardized Item Master
100+ construction items with local Hindi names mapped to standard names. No more confusion when different workers use different words for the same material (Bali → Bamboo Pole, Sariya → TMT Steel, Favda → Shovel).

👑 Admin Dashboard
Company owners can create project sites, assign site engineers, release funds, and track spending across all sites from one screen.

💰 Fund Tracking & Reconciliation
Track money given to each site engineer vs money spent. See balance per site, per engineer — daily, weekly, monthly, yearly.

✅ Entry Approval
Site engineer entries go through admin approval. Admin entries are auto-approved. Full audit trail in Google Sheets.

📊 Spending Analytics
Category-wise spending breakdown, top vendors, site-wise comparison. Filter by date range — this week, month, year, or custom dates.

🏪 Vendor & Item Management
Maintain vendor master with multi-site assignment. Add, edit, and search vendors and items. Fuzzy search finds items even with spelling variations.

👥 Multi-Site, Multi-User
Each project site has its own data sheet. Site engineers can be assigned to multiple sites. Role-based access — admin sees everything, engineers see their own entries.

🔔 Notifications
Admin gets notified on new entries. Engineers get notified on fund releases and entry approvals.

☁️ Google Sheets Backend
All data syncs to Google Sheets — your accountant can access it directly for Tally entry. No separate database needed.
